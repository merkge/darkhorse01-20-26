A Short Biography

My first real attempt to forgive my stepfather occurred around Christmas 1992. This was my first huge spiritual experience. The following year I made a decision to put aside every idea anyone ever gave me about God, and to begin my own spiritual quest from scratch. Three years later, I experienced a powerful spiritual calling.  Almost immediately thereafter, came the unexpected death of a true friend.
